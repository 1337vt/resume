More About Me

I bring 26 years of disciplined service and technical mastery to every challenge: 20 years in the military and more than a decade specializing in TTP-driven threat hunting, malware reverse engineering, and digital forensics across enterprise Windows and Unix-based ICS/SCADA environments. I’ve led and operated with teams in federally declared combat zones and across the United States, and I spent four years living fully immersed in Japanese culture — an experience that broadened my perspective, strengthened my adaptability, and deepened my respect for diverse teams and partners. My professional purpose has always been clear: protect people, systems, and critical infrastructure. I combine battlefield-tested leadership with forensic rigor to deliver investigations, threat hunting, incident response, and strategic hardening that reduce risk and restore trust. Beyond cybersecurity, I also connect people to homes. I provide trusted real estate referrals for clients who are planning moves--matching them with vetted, local agents who understand their needs so transitions are smooth and secure. If you need a seasoned digital forensics and threat-hunting leader, or a reliable real estate referral when relocation is part of the next chapter, I bring proven experience, unwavering dedication, and a commitment to results.
